# Skill: Headline Writer

Use this skill when the user asks for headlines, titles, subject lines, or hooks.

## Trigger phrases
- "write headlines", "give me headlines", "headline ideas"
- "subject line", "email subject"
- "hook", "opening line"
- "title options"
- "titres", "donne-moi des titres", "idées de titres"
- "objet d'email", "accroche", "ligne d'objet"
- "options de titres", "propose des titres"

## How to execute

Always generate **10 headlines** unless the user asks for a different number.

Use a mix of these proven frameworks — label each one so the user can see what approach it is:

**PAS (Problem → Agitate → Solution)**
Lead with a pain point the audience recognises immediately.
Example: "Still losing leads after the free trial? Here's why."

**AIDA (Attention → Interest → Desire → Action)**
Open with a bold claim, then pull the reader forward.
Example: "The homepage copy killing your conversion rate (and what to write instead)"

**Curiosity Gap**
Tease the answer without giving it away.
Example: "What the best SaaS landing pages never tell you"

**Benefit-Led**
Lead with the outcome the reader wants.
Example: "More subscribers, less effort — the welcome email that does the work"

**Specificity**
Use numbers, timeframes, and concrete details to earn trust.
Example: "7 subject lines that increased open rates by 34% (swipe file)"

**Contrarian**
Challenge a common assumption in the space.
Example: "Stop asking for the sale. Do this instead."

**Social Proof / Authority**
Borrow credibility from names, data, or consensus.
Example: "The email framework used by the top 10 Shopify brands"

**Urgency / Stakes**
Make inaction feel costly.
Example: "Every day this copy stays live, you're leaving money behind"

## Output format

Present as a numbered list. Group by framework with a short label. Respond in French. End with: "Quel angle vous semble le plus juste ? Je peux en générer d'autres dans cette direction."

## Inputs to ask for (if not provided)
- What product/service/content is the headline for?
- Who is the audience?
- What is the desired tone? (e.g. professional, playful, urgent, conversational)
- What is the goal? (clicks, opens, conversions, awareness)
